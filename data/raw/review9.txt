One of the most frequently appearing spots is Michael's Downtown, known for its wood fired pizzas, 
fresh mozzarella, and modern Italian menu.  Customers often praise the airy crust and high quality
ingredients. Little Coyote is a New York style slice shop famous for its large, foldable slices with
a crispy crust. Milana's New York Pizzeria is appreciated  for its authentic New York style pies, 
late night hours and generous portions. Parolaccia Osteria Itliana offers rustic Italian pizzas baked
in a wood fired oven with a focus on traditional flavors. 4th Horseman is known for its creative top
pings, bold flavors, and punk themed atmosphere. Customers often mention the unique combinations
offered and memorable dining experience.
